GrantSetu

India's grant engine for life sciences and biotech.

A centralized platform that connects Indian researchers with relevant government and institutional grant opportunities. GrantSetu aggregates calls from DST, DBT, ICMR, UGC, CSIR and other agencies, with daily updates and curated alerts so researchers stop missing deadlines.

PhytoSynergyDB

A curated atlas of phytochemical-antibiotic synergies.

Catalogs experimentally validated plant-derived compounds that potentiate antibiotics, with FICI values, MIC data, target organisms and proposed mechanisms. Built as a working resource for AMR research and natural-product drug discovery.

  • Python
  • Streamlit
  • Bioinformatics
  • SQLite
  • AMR
